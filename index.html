About Movies in the Barn

Movies in the Barn is a twice-monthly screening series hosted and curated by Dylan Weaver.

Each month pairs two films around a shared theme — a conversation between movies that might span decades, genres, and styles, but always find something to say to each other.

Screenings are held on Tuesdays at 7pm, twice a month.
